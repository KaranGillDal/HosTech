Our Story

Founded in 2020, HosTech is a platform designed to make the lives of patients and health professionals easy through virtual technology. The founders of HosTech had suffered from a pain of being a patient of non-curable diseases from last 18 years. With their experience as computer professionals and social workers, they had designed this platform to provide all required health needs on a single platform.
